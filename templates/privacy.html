Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information you provide directly to us. This includes your email address when you create an account, and expense data you choose to log, including categories, amounts, dates, and descriptions of your transactions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. Your expense data is used to generate summaries, charts, and insights about your spending patterns. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to outside parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting, analytics, or other operational purposes. These third parties may have access to your information only as needed to perform their functions and are obligated to maintain confidentiality.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. You can export or delete all your expense data through your account settings. If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us. We are committed to resolving any concerns you may have regarding your privacy.
